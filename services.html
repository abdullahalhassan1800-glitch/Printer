There is no single best technology — it depends on your needs. Laser excels in high-volume text printing, inkjet tank systems offer low-cost color, and multifunction devices consolidate office tasks.
Choose inkjet if you print color documents and photos infrequently. Choose laser for high-speed text printing with lower running costs. Tank printers offer the lowest cost per page for color-heavy workloads.
